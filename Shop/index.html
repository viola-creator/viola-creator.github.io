About Maana Living

Maana Living is a soulful collection of art, antiques, and everyday objects we love. The shop opened in September 2025 in Kyoto's Higashiyama district, within Maana Kiyomizu. It is inspired by the homes and quiet lifestyles of craftsmen around Japan – where living in harmony with nature and devotion to craft feel so natural and deeply human. Effortless. Tasteful.

Their stories moved us, and their pieces remind us everyday to be present. This is our way of sharing that inspiration—through objects that invite you to slow down, return to yourself, and discover beauty in the present moment.

In 2026, Maana was named one of TIME's World's Greatest Places.
