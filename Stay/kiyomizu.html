About the property

A small boutique, three ways

Tucked away in a quiet neighbourhood in eastern Kyoto, Maana Kiyomizu is a gem surrounded by ancient heritage sites, tranquil gardens, and cosy cafés. Each suite is its own machiya: serene, light-filled, and artfully intimate, with antiques and modern crafts living in harmony.

An unexpected approach to machiya stays: comfortable seating areas, deep relaxation tubs, and queen-size western beds for modern travel. All three suites carry POJ Studio's bespoke Japanese crafts, organic Senshu towels, an artisan tea set, and a seasonal bath ritual.

Above all, Kiyomizu is small. Coming home means walking past Kissa Kishin, our morning kissa, and the Maana Living shop. All three suites are next door.

Breakfast at Kissa Kishin

Your morning kissa, one door over. Kissa Kishin is a modern homage to the traditional kissaten, Japan's classic coffeehouse, serving soulful seasonal breakfasts drawn from Kyoto's farms.

Coffee, matcha, pastries, and a hearty seasonal plate. Sit at the counter or by the window. The team behind the original Kishin in Gion runs the kitchen here, exploring traditional ingredients in modern, soft ways.
