The space場所について

Hekishoken · 碧笑軒.

The ceremony unfolds inside a private tea house tucked into a quiet Kyoto neighborhood — a small, traditional room set up for chanoyu, with tatami underfoot and a hanging scroll that changes with the season. 茶の湯は、京都の静かな町並みにひっそりと佇む貸切の茶室で行われます。畳敷きの小さな伝統的な空間に、季節ごとに掛け替えられる軸が静かに迎えてくれます。

The setting is intentionally intimate. Numbers are kept small, conversation is gentle, and the room invites a slower pace than the city outside. 設えはあえて親密に。少人数で、会話は穏やかに、街の喧騒とは違うゆったりとした時間が流れます。
