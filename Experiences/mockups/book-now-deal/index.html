Three quiet ways to add it.

Same Book Now page underneath. Each variant treats the offer differently. The shared rule: small, embedded, never a banner. Click into any one to see the live mockup.

Variant A

Editorial strip across the top

A thin dateline-style band beneath the nav. Mostly small caps with one italic phrase carrying the offer. Reads as the kind of editorial framing you'd see at the top of a magazine spread. Most restrained option.

Open ↗ Variant B

Pill on the booker bar

A small terracotta pill floats on the top-left corner of the booker bar, with a quiet italic sub-line beneath. The offer is bound to the act of booking. Most direct without being loud.

Open ↗ Variant C

Editorial postcard in the corner

A small frosted-glass card pinned to the upper-right corner, with a tiny summer image, an italic headline, and the workshop bonus below. The most visual of the three, still small. Uses the summer.png you saved.

What's the same across all three. The Book Now page itself is unchanged. Only the deal element differs.

Copy in use. Primary line: "Three nights, your fourth on us." Secondary line where it fits: "10% off any workshop with your stay."

Mobile. Each variant has a small-screen behaviour tuned to keep it readable without crowding the booker.

Other surfaces. Variant A and B port directly to other pages (a workshop detail page, for example, could carry the same strip or pill). Variant C is more bespoke to the Book Now page since it depends on a hero canvas.
