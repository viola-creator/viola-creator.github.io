What's in the file. Three banner treatments at the top (paper hairline, dark inverted, two-line italic phrase). Three floating card treatments below (compact text-only, with thumbnail, dark with image background).

What changed from the earlier card. Card dimensions are smaller (320 to 360px wide instead of full tile). No italic anywhere in the cards. Each variant uses a different CTA: "Save the dates", "Book direct", "See offer".

Mobile. All banners drop the secondary "10% off workshops" segment on narrow screens to keep the offer phrase clean. All cards expand to fill row width when the viewport is under 720px.
