The space

Hekishoken · 碧笑軒.

The ceremony unfolds inside a private tea house tucked into a quiet Kyoto neighborhood — a small, traditional room set up for chanoyu, with tatami underfoot and a hanging scroll that changes with the season.

The setting is intentionally intimate. Numbers are kept small, conversation is gentle, and the room invites a slower pace than the city outside.
