Exhibition · IBASHO

For the first time in Kyoto, indigo artists Yusuke Miura (三浦裕介) and Takuma Shimada (島田拓真) will exhibit their work at Maana Atelier. Alongside the gallery, we invite you to experience indigo with the guidance of the artists using shibori techniques.

京都では初となる、SLOW FABRICの藍染作家・三浦裕介と島田拓真による展示をMaana Atelierにて開催します。会期中は、作家の手ほどきのもと、絞り染めの技法で天然藍を体験できるワークショップも開催します。

The cloth remembers where it was held. Shibori

The artists

Yusuke Miura and Takuma Shimada

三浦裕介 ・ 島田拓真

SLOW FABRIC is an indigo dye studio based in Shiga. Exploring new expression through shibori techniques, they create works that hold the traces of time and touch on fabric.

Slow Fabric studio, Shiga

The gallery

Maana Atelier, usually a by-reservation workshop space, opens its doors. With pop-up events and workshops, visitors can enter freely to experience craft and cuisine.
